AIA: Audience, Issues and Approach

Client's Name Joshua Mann, a long-time friend. Also will be the decision maker.
Business/Website Name Mann Glass Art
Redesign or New? New
Type of Website Identity, informational, ecommerce
Client Goals Showcase work, something to engage further through social media
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Art collectors: These are people who follow glass blowers on social media and collect high-end functional art. Needs:
  • to see very clear photos of each piece from different angles.
  • Details including what the names of the colors used are
  • List functional features or special color-changing materials.
  • They need a way to contact the artist.
Approach:
  • Have each beauty shot click through to a gallery of more photos
  • Detailed descriptions with time spent indicated.
  • Any personal story the artist may want to include.
Artists: These are other glass blowers and lamp workers who work with the same materials. Needs:
  • They like detailed information about each piece like the art collectors do
  • They want to engage via social media
  • Be notified about any industry events.
Approach:
  • Include a section for local events like art shows, festivals and meet-ups.
  • Make links to social media obvious.
Art dealers: These are people who own businesses or galleries that feature hand blown glass art. Needs:
  • Wholesalers need to know that they can place orders for products.
  • They will want to see what the range of items is.
Approach:
  • Include a section for local events like art shows, festivals and meet-ups.
  • Make links to social media obvious.
